About Me

Hi, I'm Paolo Prem, this is my website and I will spend just few words about myself. My passion about computers and IT in general started very early, with the few video games I had and I could play on my potato computer, which I built myself. It was made of all the stuff my father could bring me home, motherboards, RAMs, hard disks, GPUs and so on, all parts that someone was throwing away. I remember the excitement when my father was bringing me a new piece of hardware, hoping it was somehow better than the ones I had currently installed. This site is under development, as well as this page!
